What's measurable

Every "tickets" link on the site routes through a click-tracked redirect (go.html), so referral traffic to organizers and ticket platforms is a real, reportable number — not a guess. Our city pages are built to be found: server-rendered, schema.org Event markup, and a growing sitemap that already covers Paris and Edinburgh, expanding as more venues are corroborated in more cities. A Featured Listing or City Sponsor partner gets a monthly report of exactly how many clicks and impressions their placement drove — the same evidence we use to negotiate ticket-platform referral deals.
