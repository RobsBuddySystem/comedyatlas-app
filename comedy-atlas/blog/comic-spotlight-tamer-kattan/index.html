Comic Spotlight: Tamer Kattan
2026-07-17 · COMEDY ATLAS

This is our first Comic Spotlight — a new kind of post built the same way every other page on this site is built: from public data only, nothing invented, and never a fact we can't point back to a source for.

Tamer Kattan is a New York comedian who made Paris his second home. He has 159,000+ followers across social media (@tamerkat) and is a regular collaborator with Robert le Ricain, one of the early builders of Paris' English-language comedy scene. On the current circuit, he's described as a favorite on the Paris English-language stand-up scene — a reputation built over repeat appearances, not a one-off booking.

His public Paris Comedy profile lists two rooms he plays around Paris: French Fried Comedy Night and Velvet Bar — the show and the venue are the same recurring Wednesday night, run by Robert le Ricain since 2013. Full bio and profile: pariscomedy.com/c/tamer-kattan.

Why this post exists

COMEDY ATLAS's event data doesn't yet carry a lineup-level link between a specific comic and a specific upcoming date — that's an honest limit of where the data pipeline is today, not something we're going to paper over with a guessed date. So this spotlight does the thing we can actually stand behind: real biography, real socials, real recurring-room information, sourced and linked. What it deliberately does not do is promise "Tamer is on stage this Wednesday" — for the exact next date, the room's own listing or his profile page is the source of truth.

This is also a preview of where Comic Spotlights are headed: as more comics claim a public profile (opt-in only — profile public, contact never, per our privacy rule), we can build spotlights and, eventually, personalized "shows near you from comics you follow" pages directly from that consented data instead of biography text alone.
